About Me: Tim Hoheneder

Much like Anchor Away is a call beckoning to the unknown, I use photography to invite people into new, exciting places. As a former collegiate snowsports athlete I've used my background in sports to grow as a photographer. I've been lucky to have worked for clients and events including the FIS Freestyle Moguls, Aerials, and Snowboardcross World Cups, the NCAA Skiing National Championships, the World Pro Ski Tour, Kästle Skis, and the Portland Hearts of Pine.
