About Me

Hey there, my name is Tim Hoheneder! Much like Anchor Away is a call beckoning into the unknown, I use photography to bring people into new, exciting places. I came into this space through space. While pursuing my graduate degrees in satellite remote sensing, I combined my passions for travel, imagery, and sports as a former collegiate snowsports athlete. Since then, I've worked for clients and events including the FIS Freestyle Moguls, Aerials, and Snowboardcross World Cups, the NCAA Skiing National Championships, the World Pro Ski Tour, Azerbaijan Travel, Kästle Skis, and the Portland Hearts of Pine.
